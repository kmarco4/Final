Donnell Rochester, 18 years old * Baltimore, Maryland

Donnell Rochester was an 18-year-old Black male who was fatally shot by police in Baltimore, Maryland who were serving a warrant for failure to appear to a carjacking charge on February 19, 2022. Two Baltimore officers ran the tags of a Honda Accord which belonged to Donnell Rochester. The officers tried to pull Donnell over, however, Donnell drove away. Officers later discovered where Donnell had parked. Donnell and the female passenger that was with him started to run. Police officers then started to run after them when Donnell turned around to return to his car and try to drive away. As Donnell got to his car, three officers surrounded his car with their guns drawn out, ordering Donnell to get out of the vehicle. According to the footage, one officer was in front of Donnell`s car in which Donnell charged toward the officer, which resulted in the officer firing. Donnell later crashes into another car and surrenders to police. Police arrest him and later notices that he had been shot. Donnell Rochester later dies in the hospital.

According to his aunt, Markia Jackson, she stated that her nephew never drove towards the officers and that it was the officers were the ones who put themselves in front of his car then fired at her nephew. Markia stated that the police had failed to immediately take Donnell to the hospital after the shooting. "Donnell is still supposed to be here, probably just recuperating. They let him bleed out on that ground, telling him to "just breathe, bro, breathe, bro."
